Remain Nameless can trace its roots back to one fateful night in 2010...

During a weekend party with great friends, great beer and great food on a perfect August night (you know the type of summer night that weâ€™re talking about!), somebody brought an acoustic guitar outside. Inevitably, the guitar was passed around our circle of friends sitting around the fire pit, and our unplugged set of Nirvana, Metallica, Smashing Pumpkins and Foo Fighters accompanied our conversation for hours.

That night eventually led to the idea of a jam session, which led to weekly jam sessions between Brian and Kim with other friends joining in occasionally. Fast forward to the summer of 2011, and the momentum of starting a cover band was in full swing. Not being able to decide on an appropriate band name, we decided to "remain nameless" as we started to play gigs, parties and events.
